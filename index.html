Top Ten places to eat near WSU

  1. Roosters (Their brunch is killer.)
  2. Harp and Hound (Bias due to working there. That's also why it's the only one with a link.)
  3. McDonald's (Not really that good I'm just cheap.)
  4. Virg's (Only have an hour between classes come over here.)
  5. Einstein's Bagels (Good for a meal or a sweet treat.)
  6. Jack in the Box (I mean it's 99 cents for two tacos come on.)
  7. Ruan Thai (Bit far out of the way from campus, but some of my favorite Thai in all of Ogden.)
  8. The Pie Pizzeria (This one is an institution. Been around since my mom went here and still going strong.)
  9. Waldo's Grill (You have fifteen minutes till an exam and need something right now type of food.)
  10. Waldo's Conveince (Same as the last one, but you only got five minutes and need a monster and food.)
